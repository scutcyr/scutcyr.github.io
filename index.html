Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate with School of Eletronic and Information Engieering, South China University of Technology, China, where I obtained my bachelor degree. My research interest mainly lies in Natural Language Processing(NLP), Computer Vision(CV), Deep Learning(DL), image processing and Dialogue system.

I am advised by Prof. Xiangmin Xu in SCUT and co-advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao in USYD. I am so lucky to work closely with Prof. Kui Jia in UM.
