Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate with School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology, China, where I obtained my bachelor degree. My research interest mainly lies in computer vision, deep learning, image processing and visual tracking.

I am advised by Prof. Xiangmin Xu in SCUT and co-advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao in USYD. I am so lucky to work closely with Prof. Kui Jia in UM.

He has twice received three good students from South China University of Technology, “Outstanding Communist Youth League Member” in scut, and XinMade First Class Scholarship (3000 yuan, 3rd comprehensive test), Huawei Scholarship (5000 yuan, 3rd comprehensive test), Huiding Science and Technology Scholarship (15,000 yuan, 1st comprehensive test), won the first prize of American College Students Mathematical Modeling Contest (MCM) twice, won 2016 The second prize of the National College Student Mathematical Modeling Contest (Guangdong Division), the first prize of the 2017 National College Student Mathematical Modeling Contest (Guangdong Division), the first prize of the 2018 Guangdong University Student Electronic Design Competition, etc. 2017-2019 National College Student Innovation Training Program, participated in two Guangdong University students' science and technology innovation training funds, a 2018-2019 national college student innovation training program, and three South China University of Technology "Hundred Steps Ladder Plan" project
